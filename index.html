despelote is a soccer game where the rules aren’t enforced by the game, but socially by the people around you.

Run, pass, and play with dozens of charming, hand-drawn characters as you learn the language of soccer and explore the streets and parks of Quito, set against the backdrop of Ecuador's first qualifying run for the World Cup in 2002.
